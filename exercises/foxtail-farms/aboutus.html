A Fox Tale

The Farmers Market is dynamic outdoor venue that connects local producers and artisans with the community. In addition to food and artisanal crafts, the culture of the market includes live music and other market happenings such as yoga, art and dance classes that are an integral part of the Fox Tale community. Turning an earthly mission into a bright vision, The Foxtail Market invites you to join us with your friends and family to support and nurture our beloved community.
